Right-clicking on a Bookmarks link while left-click is down couses the context menu to stay open after left-click is up.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open bookmarks menu,
2. Hold down left-click on a bookmark link, and then press the right-click,
3. Release left-click.

Actual Results:  
Context menu still available.

Expected Results:  
Context menu should be hidden.
