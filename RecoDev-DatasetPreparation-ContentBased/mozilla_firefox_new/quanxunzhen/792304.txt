> BTW, why are Obj-C exceptions not fatal?

Sometimes they are and sometimes they aren't.  It depends on where in OS code they happen.  Though usually in my experience they're not fatal.

For a while we deliberately made them all fatal (or as many as we had control over), in connection with our work on bug 163260.  But this was a terrible mistake and was eventually rectified.  Likewise our work on bug 163260 turned out to be completely misguided.
