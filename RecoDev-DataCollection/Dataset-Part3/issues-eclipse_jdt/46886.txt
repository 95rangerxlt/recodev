-> UI thread owns lock on CompilationUnit, and is waiting for the builder to
complete

-> Builder thread (Worker-31) is waiting for lock on CompilationUnit (in

CompilationUnit.

Editors have to either avoid modifying the workspace while holding the CU
monitor, or avoid acquiring the monitor from the resource change listener.
