I20041221-0800

Examples:
@interface A {}
@ interface B {}

The "@" is not rendered keyword-style unless there's no whitespace between "@"
and "interface". For consistency, the "@" should always be rendered in the same
style. 

Although the JLS does not count "@" as keyword, I opt for rendering it as keyword.
