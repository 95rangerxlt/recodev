fix

The factory should be returning JavaWatchExpressionFilter - which is our watch expression factory.

I also found that we allowed you to make a watch expression for an index variable partition which will never work because you wind up with expressions named: "[0-99]" which cannot be resolved.

I was unable to reproduce the actual CCE, and I also could not have the adapter factory be asked for a watch factory for a JavaInspectExpression, perhaps because I also have a fix for bug 384928 in my workspace.
pushed fix to: http://git.eclipse.org/c/jdt/eclipse.jdt.debug.git/commit/?id=36707665b54f2739c710052e67230df85ff51931

the commit also corrects the adapter list reported by the factory
