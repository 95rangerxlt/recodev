On every mouse move, autoscroll is shortly interrupted.
Therefore scrolling is not smooth while adjusting the speed.
This is slightly annoying and not beneficial to the user experience.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.invoke autoscroll on a suitably long page, e.g.  http://slashdot.org/
2.set some speed
3.move mouse slightly
4.observe jerky scrolling
Actual Results:  
Scrolling is interrupted or prevented by mouse moving.

Expected Results:  
Smooth scrolling.
